COMMON CUSTOMER QUESTIONS
--------------------------
Q: Can I import data from Asana or Trello?
A: Yes. Go to Settings → Import → choose Asana or Trello, then authorize with OAuth. Import takes 5-30 minutes depending on data size.

Q: Is there a mobile app?
A: Yes. AcmeFlow is available on iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play). All features except Gantt chart editing are available on mobile.

Q: What integrations do you support?
A: Native integrations: Slack, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, GitHub, Jira, Zapier, Salesforce. API available for custom integrations.

Q: Is my data encrypted?
A: All data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). SOC 2 Type II certified. GDPR compliant. Data stored in AWS us-east-1 (US customers) and AWS eu-west-1 (EU customers).

Q: Can I have multiple workspaces?
A: Pro and Enterprise plans support unlimited workspaces. Starter is limited to 1 workspace.

Q: How do I add team members?
A: Settings → Team → Invite Members. Enter email addresses, assign roles (Admin/Member/Viewer). Invitations expire after 7 days.
